Impressive Feats of Engineering

One of the oldest and most important applications of engineering was in warfare. Engineers pioneered the road of advancing mechanics, themodynamics, and electronics into world-changing designs. Much of civilian technology actually is derived from their military applications. Although some of these machines and concepts are now considered obsolete, they were revolutionary for their time and deserve to be known and respected by all future engineers.

As a Computer Engineer, everything here impresses me because I could never think of these mechanical solutions or even concepts let alone build working prototypes and use them to set records.

I hope you are fascinated by these inventions as much as I am.
